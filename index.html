About KOMU

The history and vision of Kingsley Ozumba Mbadiwe University.

🏛️
Founded and Renamed 2021
Originally Eastern Palm University (2016), renamed KOMU on 18th February 2021 under Imo State Law No. 1 of 2021 by Governor Hope Uzodimma.
📍
Location
Ogboko, Ideato South Local Government Area, Imo State, Nigeria.
🎖️
NUC Recognition
Officially the 42nd State University and 143rd in the entire Nigerian University System.
👤
Named After a Legend
Named in honour of Dr. K.O. Mbadiwe — nationalist and government minister of Nigeria's First Republic from Imo State.
🌍
Vision
"To be a globally competitive University renowned for innovative research, world-class education and excellent human resource development."
